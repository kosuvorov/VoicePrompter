# VoicePrompter or BIGVU: A Focused Teleprompter vs an All-in-One Suite

BIGVU and [VoicePrompter](https://apps.apple.com/app/apple-store/id6758573080?pt=128503212&ct=blog-vs-bigvu&mt=8) solve the teleprompter problem from opposite directions. BIGVU is a **creator suite** - teleprompter plus captions, editing, and publishing in one place. VoicePrompter is a **focused, native Mac teleprompter** that does prompting better than a suite can and stays out of the way of everything else. The right pick depends on whether you want one tool that does many things, or the best tool for the one thing.

## Two different philosophies

**BIGVU** is an all-in-one video kit. If you want to record, auto-caption, trim, brand, and publish short videos without leaving one app, it covers a lot of ground - mostly aimed at social/marketing video, and largely web- and mobile-based.

**VoicePrompter** is deliberately narrow. It's a native Mac overlay that floats on top of any app, stays **invisible during screen sharing**, and follows your voice with real **word tracking** in 60+ languages. It doesn't edit or publish your video - it makes the *reading* effortless and gets out of the way, so it pairs with whatever recording or conferencing tools you already use.

## Side by side

| | VoicePrompter | BIGVU |
|---|---------------|-------|
| Scope | **Focused teleprompter** | All-in-one suite (captions, editing, publishing) |
| Voice scrolling | **Word tracking + Sound mode** | Teleprompter with auto-scroll/voice |
| Invisible on screen share | **Yes** | No |
| Native Mac app | **Yes** | Web / mobile |
| Best for | Demos, calls, courses, podcasts on Mac | All-in-one social video production |

## Where BIGVU makes sense

If your real goal is producing captioned social clips end to end - and you'd rather have captions, editing, and scheduling bundled with your teleprompter - BIGVU's breadth is the point. For that workflow, an all-in-one suite can genuinely save you from juggling apps.

**Does BIGVU have a teleprompter?** Yes - it's one feature of a broader suite that also does captions, editing, and publishing.
